The three pillars — utility triad as research programme
AU · Analytic Utility

What signal can a pipeline extract?

Infrastructure for maximising the information yield of multimodal corpora. Theory-driven and data-driven knowledge creation as complements, not opposites.

  • SMASH v2 (canonical event log architecture)
  • EnvisionBOX gesture classification challenge
  • Cross-corpus comparison at scale
  • Non-Western corpora: Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo speech
RU · Reflexive Utility

What does masking cost?

Making de-identification costs legible. Privacy as a principled design choice, not a binary compliance toggle. Community consent models for non-Western field sites.

  • MaskBench (88–95% pose quality under blur)
  • REDES Surveillance Sandbox
  • SYNAPSIS provenance schema
  • Community-based consent in Nigerian contexts
GU · Generative Utility

What can be synthesized — and should it?

Synthetic corpora, model training, cross-modal generation. Highest governance demand. Especially urgent for underrepresented populations where real data is scarce and synthetic data could compensate — or harm.

  • JEGAL (cross-modal embedding substrate)
  • LEGO: child–AI coupling, on-device small LLM
  • SLlama: compact models for low-resource settings
  • Deepfake governance for non-Western contexts
