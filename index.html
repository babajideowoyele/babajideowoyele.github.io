Projects

SMASH

NWO Funded · €50K 2024–2025

Synthesis and Multimodal Analytics System for Humanities

Fully open-source tool that analyzes non-verbal communication — body language, facial expressions, and speech patterns. Integrates privacy-preserving data archiving with behavioral analysis, making multimodal communication research accessible to non-programmers.

Capabilities

  • Hand gesture analysis & diversity metrics
  • Facial expression & posture tracking
  • Voice tone variation analysis
  • Automatic speech transcription

Impact

  • Cross-cultural speaker databases
  • Clinical research applications
  • Social sciences & humanities tool
  • Privacy-first data archiving
Multimodal AI Computer Vision NLP Privacy Tech Open Source

Collaboration: Radboud University, Hasso Plattner Institute · Co-investigators: Gerard de Melo, Wim Pouw · Radboud project page

SYNAPSIS

NWO Funded · €250K PI 2024–2026

Privacy-Preserving Audiovisual Data Sharing for Research

TDCC-SSH Challenge Project enabling researchers to share audiovisual data safely. Combines the MaskAnyone toolkit with SURF cloud infrastructure and researcher training to make privacy-preserving tools accessible across Dutch universities.

Capabilities

  • Face & voice masking for GDPR compliance
  • Body kinematics extraction
  • SURF cloud integration
  • Masking School training workshops

Impact

  • 100+ trainees across Dutch universities
  • 7 institutional partners
  • DANS long-term data archiving
  • Open science sustainability model
Privacy Tech Open Science Training GDPR

Partners: VU Amsterdam, UvA, EUR, University of Groningen, Tilburg University, SURF, DANS
