Featured Projects

SMASH

2024-2025 • NWO Funded

Synthesis and Multimodal Analytics System for Humanities

Leading development of a fully open-source tool that analyzes non-verbal communication - body language, facial expressions, and speech patterns. SMASH integrates privacy-preserving data archiving with behavioral analysis, making multimodal communication research accessible to non-programmers.

Capabilities
  • • Hand gesture analysis & diversity metrics
  • • Facial expression & posture tracking
  • • Voice tone variation analysis
  • • Automatic speech transcription
Impact
  • • Cross-cultural speaker databases
  • • Clinical research applications
  • • Social sciences & humanities tool
  • • Privacy-first data archiving
Multimodal AI Computer Vision NLP Privacy Tech Open Source

Collaboration: Radboud University, Hasso Plattner Institute • Co-investigators: Gerard de Melo, Wim Pouw
